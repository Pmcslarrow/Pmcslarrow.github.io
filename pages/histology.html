Summary

This project aimed to classify cell morphology (21 classes) in histology images from the Bone-Marrow-Cytomorphology dataset, using 20% of the data due to its large size. The team first established a baseline using KNN, then developed a CNN in PyTorch to improve performance, employing techniques like batch normalization and dropout for optimization. The solution is designed as a supportive tool to enhance doctorsâ€™ confidence in pursuing additional diagnostic tests, not as a replacement for medical expertise. While the results were not perfect, they significantly outperformed random guessing, showing promise as an aid in hematologic disease diagnosis.
